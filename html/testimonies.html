A remarkable testimony of God's divine intervention unfolded when a woman, seven months pregnant, received devastating news — her baby had been declared dead, and doctors at Warri Central Hospital planned a medical procedure to remove it.

However, after prayers from the man of God, a miraculous turn of events occurred. That very night, at Ekpan General Hospital in Effurun, Delta State, the baby was brought back to life, and a premature birth took place. Both mother and child survived, healthy and strong—a powerful testament to God's mercy and power.

All glory to God for this incredible miracle!
